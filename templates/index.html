Why Hybrid Phrases?

Hybrid passphrases combine the high-entropy unpredictability of randomly selected dictionary words with the complexity of symbols and numbers. Unlike complex random strings, they leverage the brain's natural capacity for storytelling and spatial recall, making them significantly harder to crack via brute force while remaining human-memorable.
